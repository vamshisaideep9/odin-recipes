Biryani Recipe

Description

Hyderabad's Most Favorite dish. Dum Biryani is known for its taste and flavor.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Curd
  • All Types of spices
  • Garam Masala
  • Lemons
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Chilli Powder
  • Onions
  • Chillis
  • Ghee
  • Ginger Paste

Steps:

  1. Take a pan and fry onions
  2. Take chicken in the bowl and add chilli powder, turmeric, ginger garlic paste, fried onions, curd
  3. Mix the bowl and let it soak for 2 hours
  4. Take basmati rice and clean the rice with water
  5. Add 2-3 cups of fresh water in the basmati rice and put it on the stove until the rice is cooked.
